# Amazon Prohibited Products Policy

## Introduction
The following products are prohibited from sale on Amazon.

## Illegal and Potentially Illegal Products
Products sold on Amazon must comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

## Drugs and Drug Paraphernalia
- Controlled substances, such as cannabidiol (CBD), are prohibited.
- Products that simulate the effects of controlled substances.
- Equipment designed for making or using drugs.

## Hazardous and Dangerous Items
- Explosives, fireworks, and flares.
- Radioactive materials.
- Mercury-added products (with some exceptions like batteries).

## Medical Devices and Accessories
- Products that require a prescription or a medical professional's supervision.
- Products that have been recalled by the FDA.
- Professional-use only dental products.

## Offensive and Controversial Materials
- Products that promote hatred, violence, or discrimination.
- Products that promote or glorify suicide.

## Weapons
- Firearms, ammunition, and silencers.
- Switchblade knives and other illegal knives.
- Tear gas and pepper spray (restrictions apply by state).

## Intellectual Property Violations
- Counterfeit products.
- Replicas or "knock-offs" of branded products.
- "Dupe" listings that claim to be similar to a famous brand.
